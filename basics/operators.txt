Logical Operators:

  ! (logical NOT)
  || (logical OR)
  && (logical AND)

Bitwise Operators:

  & (bitwise AND)
  | (bitwise OR)
  ^ (bitwise XOR)
  &^ (bit clear, AND NOT)
  << (left shift)
  >> (right shift)

Comparison Operators:

  == (equal)
  != (not equal)
  < (less than)
  <= (less than or equal to)
  > (greater than)
  >= (greater than or equal to)
